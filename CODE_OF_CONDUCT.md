# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We expect all participants in this project to:

- Communicate respectfully and professionally
- Accept constructive criticism and give it in good faith
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not individuals
- Be inclusive and welcoming to contributors regardless of background
- Acknowledge the contributions of others

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Deliberate intimidation or hostile communication
- Publishing private information about others without consent
- Disruptive behavior that derails technical discussion

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or other participation that violates this code of conduct, and may ban contributors whose behavior is persistently harmful.

Enforcement decisions are made at the maintainers' discretion with the goal of maintaining a constructive and professional environment.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainers directly. Reports will be reviewed confidentially.

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within all project spaces, including the GitHub repository, issue tracker, pull requests, and any other community communication associated with this project.
